About BFORT

A small, owner-run wires and cables workshop — ten skilled workers, one manager and an owner who answers his own phone.

Our Story

A small workshop that takes wiring seriously

BFORT began with a simple belief: electricity should be safe by design. We're a proprietor-run workshop — small on headcount, careful on standards.

We keep our range focused and our process hands-on. Being small means nothing ships without someone experienced actually looking at it — and when you call, you reach a decision-maker, not a department.

  • Every batch checked before dispatch — nothing ships unseen.
  • Careful workmanship for consistent conductor sizing and finish.
  • Local dealer network built on repeat customers and word of mouth.
