Our Story

Three decades of powering progress

Founded on the conviction that electricity should be safe by design, BFORT has grown from a small drawing unit into a fully‑integrated manufacturer of wires and cables trusted across the country.

We control every step — from purifying copper and drawing conductors to insulating, sheathing and testing the finished cable. That vertical integration is why our quality stays consistent, batch after batch, year after year.

Today BFORT cables carry power through homes, factories, solar farms and infrastructure projects — quietly doing their job, exactly as engineered.

Our Mission

To deliver wires and cables that set the benchmark for safety and reliability, making quality electrical infrastructure accessible to every home and industry.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in cabling — recognised for engineering excellence, uncompromising standards and genuine care for the people we power.
