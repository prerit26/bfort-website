Our Story

A small team that sweats the details

Founded on the conviction that electricity should be safe by design, BFORT is a small, proprietor‑run unit: ten skilled workers on the floor, one manager keeping things moving, and an owner who stands behind every order.

We keep our range focused and our process hands‑on. Being small means nothing ships without someone experienced actually looking at it — and when you call, you reach a decision‑maker, not a department.

BFORT cables power homes, shops and workshops — quietly doing their job, exactly as intended.

Our Mission

To make safe, reliable wires and cables that ordinary homes and businesses can afford — and to stand behind every metre we sell.

Our Vision

To grow slowly and honestly — earning trust one customer, one electrician and one dealer at a time.
